About Us

We were tired of our friends only suggesting ideas that involved drinking and eating. What ever happened to active ideas like when we were kids. No kid ever came to your house and said "Hey do you want to sit and eat grilled cheese?" No! It was always, "Do you want to come over and play soccer, basketball, spy, etc." So we built an application that will let you regain your childhood creativity and get moving with your friends. You'll have more fun, your friends will appreciate it, and you'll be in better shape!
